About

Welcome to my professional portfolio! My name is Bryan Tempini, and I am a software developer with a diverse background that has shaped my approach to problem-solving and technology.

During my seven-year tenure as a master Harley Davidson technician, I honed my skills in attention to detail, critical thinking, and effective communication. These experiences instilled in me the importance of precision and reliability, qualities that I now bring to my work as a web developer.

In addition to my technical expertise, I have spent the last six months as a teacher's assistant for the University of Utah's coding BootCamp. This role has allowed me to further refine my abilities in mentoring and guiding aspiring developers, fostering an environment of continuous learning and growth.

I have also had the privilege of serving as a project manager and front-end developer for a startup company, where I contributed to the development of a full-stack application tailored for the equine industry. This experience provided me with valuable insights into project management, client collaboration, and the intricacies of creating intuitive user interfaces.

In this portfolio, you will find a selection of my skills and projects that showcase my passion for leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I am always eager to collaborate, learn, and contribute to innovative projects. Please feel free to reach out with any questions or opportunities for collaboration.
